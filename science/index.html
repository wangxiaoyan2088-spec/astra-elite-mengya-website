PART 3 — SCIENCE TIME

Why can drones fly?

Drones fly because spinning propellers push air downward. When the air moves down, the drone moves up. This is called lift.

无人机能够飞行，是因为高速旋转的螺旋桨会把空气向下推动。空气向下时，无人机就会获得向上的力量。这种力量叫：升力 Lift。

这种力量叫：升力 Lift
The propellers spin fast! Air goes down. Drone goes up!
