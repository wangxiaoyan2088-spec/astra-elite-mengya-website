The Moon Signal

Leta was not supposed to touch the red button on the giant silver drone. But she touched it anyway. BEEEEEP! The classroom lights flashed, the drone lifted into the air, and a giant hologram Moon appeared above everyone.

WHY STORY LEARNING?

故事驱动英语、科学和想象力

孩子不是先背概念，而是先进入一个问题：为什么无人机飞起来？Miro 为什么紧张？月亮信号要带大家去哪里？当孩子想知道答案时，英语词汇、科学解释和表达任务就自然出现。
